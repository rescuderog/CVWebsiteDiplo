Database and visualization utility of the PICTO-UCA Project 2017-0032

This database was built in collaboration with the researcher team of the project, and consisted in a collection of all judicial decisions made by the Interamerican Court of Human Rights. One of my main tasks was to organize, sanitize and build upon the initial information collected. Then, I converted that information into an SQLite database, and developed a custom visualization tool. That tool was an integral part of the project, as it helped to better visualize the information and generate different reports and graphics.
